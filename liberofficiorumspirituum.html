Liber Officiorum Spirituum

Paimon

The Liber Officiorum Spirituum is a lesser-known grimoire that details various spirits and their ranks. It includes Paimon, who is described as a king and is noted for his obedience to Lucifer. In this text, Paimon is first listed as the sixth spirit and later as the third king.
