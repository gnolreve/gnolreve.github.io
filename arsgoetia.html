King Paimon

The Ars Goetia, the first section of the Lesser Key of Solomon, is one of the most well-known grimoires in demonology. It lists 72 demons, including Paimon, who is described as a powerful King of Hell. - Rank: King - Legions: Commands 200 legions of spirits - Appearance: A man with an effeminate face, wearing a crown, and riding a dromedary camel - Accompaniment: Arrives with a loud procession of musicians, playing trumpets and cymbals - Abilities: Grants knowledge, wisdom, and secrets, teaches arts, philosophy, and sciences, and can bind people to the conjurers will - Summoning Ritual: The conjurer must face northwest, the direction of Paimonâ€™s domain. Paimon is considered more obedient to Lucifer than other kings and is often accompanied by two additional spirits, Labal and Abalam. If summoned alone, he may require offerings or sacrifices
